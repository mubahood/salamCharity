What is Zakat

Zakat is alms (religious tax) for the poor and needy in our society. It is one of the five pillars of Islam, which include declaration of faith (Shahada), prayer (Swalah), fasting in Ramadan (Saum) and the pilgrimage to Mecca during the prescribe time (Hajj). Zakah is obligatory upon every adult Muslim of sound mind and means.

Zakat is payable on attaining a specific amount of wealth or savings which is referred to as Nisaab. The amount of Zakat to be paid is 2.5% of Nisaab.
