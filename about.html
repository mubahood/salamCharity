Who we are

Salam Charity was created under the Salam Television to handle the Charity wing for Ugandans and the rest of the East African Community. Salam Television was found founded in 2014 with the aim of helping to fight poverty and transform lives. Every night over 800 million people go hungry and nearly 6 million children die each year from preventable diseases. Salam Charity Foundation has come up to stand with the people in need .

Salam Charity aims to tackle this by providing lifesaving meals, strategic education, health, clothing, and livelihood. This will allow everyone to realize their birth right of living a good life by developing their full potential.

Salam Charity works in many parts of the Country in partnership with the Government of Uganda, credible international and local implementing partners. We are innovative, transparent and digitally driven.
